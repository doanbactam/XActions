Why it runs in your browser

Follow, unfollow, and like actions need your logged-in X session. A hosted service would have to store your session token on a server and drive your account from a datacenter — which puts your account at risk and trips X's automation detection. The extension avoids all of that: it acts inside your own browser session, on your own IP, so the actions look exactly like you using X normally. We never see your credentials.

Install

Load it in 30 seconds

Chrome Edge Brave Firefox
1

Download the code

Clone or download the XActions repository — the extension lives in the extension/ folder.

2

Open your extensions page

Go to chrome://extensions/ (or edge://extensions/ / brave://extensions/). On Firefox, use about:debugging#/runtime/this-firefox.

3

Enable Developer mode & load unpacked

Toggle Developer mode (top-right), click Load unpacked, and select the extension/ folder. On Firefox, click Load Temporary Add-on and pick extension/manifest.json.

4

Pin it and open x.com

The XA icon appears in your toolbar. Navigate to x.com — a green dot means it's connected. Click the icon, pick an automation, and hit ▶.
